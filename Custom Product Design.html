Custom Product Design


We specialize in crafting product designs, from unique objects to statement décor that elevate your spaces beyond conventional boundaries. Thought through to seamlessly integrate into your design narrative, each element reflects your individuality. Our collaborative approach ensures that every object not only enhances visual appeal but also serves a meaningful purpose in your daily life.

In-Depth Consultation:

• Engage in a comprehensive discussion to understand your preferences, lifestyle, and specific requirements.
• Collaborate on establishing a design direction that aligns with your vision and the overall aesthetic of your space.

Customized Product Curation:

• Immerse yourself in a curated selection of high-quality and unique furnishings, decor, and accessories tailored to your taste.
• Benefit from our extensive network of suppliers, ensuring access to exclusive and artisanal pieces that elevate your space.

Material and Finish Selection:

• Receive expert guidance on materials and finishes for furniture and accessories, considering durability, functionality, and visual appeal.
• Explore a range of options that align with your design goals, from luxurious fabrics to sustainable and eco-friendly choices.

Budget Management:

• Transparent budget discussions and meticulous planning to ensure your project stays within the agreed-upon financial parameters.
• Leverage our industry knowledge to source products that offer exceptional value without compromising on quality or style.

Project Coordination:

• Seamless coordination with suppliers, manufacturers, and artisans to guarantee timely delivery and installation of chosen products.
• Regular updates and communication throughout the process, providing you with peace of mind and confidence in the progress of your project.

Quality Assurance:

• Rigorous quality checks on all procured items to ensure they meet our high standards and your expectations.
• Addressing any issues promptly and efficiently to guarantee your complete satisfaction with the final result.

Post-Installation Support:

• Beyond the completion of your project, enjoy ongoing support for any adjustments, additions, or inquiries regarding the products chosen for your space.
• Our commitment to your satisfaction extends well beyond the installation phase, reinforcing a lasting relationship.

Frequently Asked Questions


What is the initial process for engaging in your Product Design service?

We begin with a complimentary discovery call to understand your vision, preferences, and specific requirements. This sets the foundation for a collaborative design journey tailored to your unique style.

How do you ensure the products selected align with my vision and aesthetic preferences?

Our Product Design service involves personalized design concepts, mood boards, and 3D renderings to visualize the proposed design. We work closely with you, allowing for customization based on your feedback and preferences.

Can I provide input on the materials and finishes for the selected products?

Absolutely. We provide expert guidance on materials and finishes, considering factors like durability, functionality, and visual appeal. Your preferences play a pivotal role in curating a collection that aligns with your design goals.

How do you manage the budget for the Product Design project?

We believe in transparent budget discussions and meticulous planning. Leveraging our industry knowledge, we source products that offer exceptional value without compromising on quality or style.

What does the project coordination involve, and how do you ensure timely delivery?

Seamless coordination with suppliers, manufacturers, and artisans is a key aspect. Regular updates and communication throughout the process provide you with peace of mind, ensuring timely delivery and installation.

What quality assurance measures do you have in place for procured items?

Rigorous quality checks are conducted on all procured items to meet our high standards. Any issues are promptly addressed to guarantee your complete satisfaction with the final result.

Is there post-installation support provided for adjustments or additions?

Beyond project completion, we offer ongoing support for any adjustments, additions, or inquiries regarding the products chosen for your space. Our commitment extends well beyond the installation phase, fostering a lasting relationship.
