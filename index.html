Welcome to The Moodie Studio

We are a multidisciplinary design studio in the business of creativity. Moodie Studio is the brainchild of Evelyn Martinez, a seasoned designer with a Bachelor of Architecture and Minor in Marketing from the University of Southern California. Evelyn’s rich 9-year experience in architecture, interior design, and custom furniture design across the counties of Los Angeles, San Bernardino, Orange and San Diego, form the backbone of the studio.

Our portfolio is diverse, encompassing residences, commercial spaces, real estate developments, and furniture. What distinguishes Moodie Studio is our approach to design - a patient process that puts people at the heart of every decision. Design here is not just about aesthetics, it’s about crafting spaces that resonate with those who inhabit them, fostering a good mood and positivity. We’re transparent in our dealings and genuine in our intent. The goal is not just to meet expectations, but to exceed them. It’s not just about delivering projects, it’s about delivering value.

Our Studio stands for authenticity. It’s about crafting spaces that are as practical as they are aesthetically pleasing. Spaces that remain thoughtful under the test of time. Spaces that you’ll love to inhabit.

Evelyn Martinez, Founder
