A 3D Printed Product Made from Recycled Materials

Durian Planter


RENDERS


The Durian planter is one of the products in a collection of planters that I created as an artist who has a passion for horticulture and industrial design. The collection of planters is a result of my exploration of a new path in artistry, using 3D printing technology and recycled materials.

It is inspired by the exotic fruit of the same name, and has a dumpling-like shape with deep ridges. Made from recycled composite wood material which is biodegradable and renewable, 3D printed using a process that minimizes waste and energy consumption. The planter is also resistant and durable, and can be used indoor or in covered exterior spaces in commercial or residential environments. It comes in different colors that can match different identities and style.

The product is designed to provide optimal conditions for your plants’ health and growth. It comes in a large, medium, and small size to fit different plant sizes. A drainage outlet is located at the base of the product to protect plant roots against any infestation or rot. The base of the planter has a built-in mesh to contain any small gravel that you would like to add for added drainage quality and additionaly includes a base plate for the product to sit upon. The plate is used to collect excess water that leaks from the planter, and can be easily removed and cleaned.

The Durian planter is a product that reflects my vision and creativity as an artist. It is also a product that demonstrates my commitment to environmental sustainability and function as a designer. It is a product that I hope you will enjoy having in your home or office.
